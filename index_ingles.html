Description

What can I do with the Tutoshop¬¥s plugin

Live Commerce or Shopstreaming, call it what you want! LiveComm Tutorshop is a plugin that brings the physical shopping experience to the online world. You interact with your customers in real time, via live and chat, and sell your products and/or services on the same screen.


The shopper watches the live on its website, interacts via chat, and can buy the products presented without having to leave the live screen, as the selected goods are displayed below the live, with a purchase option.


Made for any type of store and category: cosmetics, clothes, services, games, bazaar, whatever you want!


Sell more by interacting with your customers and facilitating the sale of your products.


This is the first version. It's a beta version, please give us your feedback so we can improve.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions

  • What is shopstreaming?

    Shopstreaming is the use of live streaming technology for the purpose of online sales. In this way, while a salesperson is showing and talking about a certain product, the consumer can interact, ask questions, and, in a few clicks, make their purchase in a few clicks.

  • Are you a streaming?

    No, we mirror your live video using the main streams, so you record from your twitch ;or facebook or youtube account and the video appears in real time on your platform.

  • How does the chat work?

    The person can either send questions to you through your website screen, or directly in the streamings, we compile everything into a single chat, but the ideal is to always call all customers to only one communication channel, your site, where they can buy the products just below the live.

  • How do I select the products that I want to appear on the live?

    You create a category, like any other and add the products you want to your live, we advise you to call the category created "live" or something similar, it is important you edit the category every time you do a different live, if you want to change the products.

  • How do I schedule the live?

    Click on Dashbord ü°™ Settings ü°™ LiveComm Tutorshop, a screen will open for you to add your live commerce. You must fill in the fields listed on the live screen, which are the same as below:
    1. Stream title
    2. Stream Platform = Choose which stream you want to use, twitch or facebook
    3. Live Url = Link of the live
    4. Product Category = Category created with the products for the live
    5. Live date = Day and time of the live

  • How do I mirror Twitch video on the platform?

    Just schedule a live normally on the Twitch platform, and register your user in your e-commerce when registering the live. For example if your user is: LINDO123, you just put LINDO123 in the tab Live Url at the time of registration of the live...

How to proceed
Here is a short tutorial

  •  Automatic installation — Step 1

    Automatic installation is the easiest option - WordPress will take care of the file transfer and you don't need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic installation of LiveComm Tutorshop, log in to the WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu and click "Add New".

  •  Automatic installation — Step 2

    In the search field, type "LiveComm Tutorshop" and click "Search plug-ins". After you find us, you can see details about it, such as rating and description. Most importantly, of course, you can install it there Click "Install Now" and WordPress will do it from there.

  •  Manual installation — Single step

    The WordPress Codex contains instructions on how to do this. You can find a guide in the menu at the top of the page.

  •  Manual installation — Help

    If you encounter problems with your store/category pages after an upgrade, release the permalinks by going to WordPress > Settings > Permalinks and clicking "Save". This should return things to normal.
